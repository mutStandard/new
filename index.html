How We Build

Four layers, poured in order.

−3.0MFoundation
0.0MStructure
+4.5MEnvelope
+8.0MFinish
  • −3.0M

    Foundation

    Reinforced footings, poured to depth and left to cure before anything else happens on site.

  • 0.0M

    Structure

    A concrete core or steel frame — whichever carries the load most honestly for that site.

  • +4.5M

    Envelope

    Facade, glazing and weatherproofing, sized to the climate rather than to a standard catalogue.

  • +8.0M

    Finish

    Floors, fixtures, final inspection. The building is signed off, not just switched on.
